# Business Continuity Policy

## 1. Purpose
Ensure critical business functions continue during and after a disruption.

## 2. Key Definitions
- RTO: Maximum acceptable downtime
- RPO: Maximum acceptable data loss
- MTPD: Maximum tolerable period of disruption

## 3. Requirements
- BIA conducted annually
- BCP tested semi-annually
- DR site maintained
- Communication plan established

## 4. Critical Functions
Identified through Business Impact Analysis with assigned RTOs and RPOs.
