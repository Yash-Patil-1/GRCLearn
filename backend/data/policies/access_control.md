# Access Control Policy

## 1. Purpose
Define requirements for managing access to organizational information systems and data.

## 2. Scope
All employees, contractors, and third parties with access to organizational systems.

## 3. Policy Statements

### 3.1 Account Management
- All access requires formal approval from the data/system owner.
- Accounts must be uniquely identifiable to an individual.
- Shared accounts are prohibited except where technically required and approved.
- Accounts must be reviewed quarterly.
- Terminated user accounts must be disabled within 24 hours.

### 3.2 Least Privilege
- Access shall be granted based on the principle of least privilege.
- Users receive only the minimum access necessary for their role.
- Privileged access requires additional approval and monitoring.

### 3.3 Authentication
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all remote access.
- MFA is required for all privileged accounts.
- Passwords must meet complexity requirements (14+ characters).
- Account lockout after 5 failed attempts.

### 3.4 Remote Access
- Remote access requires VPN with MFA.
- All remote sessions must be encrypted.
- Remote access is logged and monitored.

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities
- **IT Security**: Enforce policy, conduct access reviews
- **System Owners**: Approve access requests
- **Managers**: Validate employee access needs
- **Users**: Protect credentials, report unauthorized access

## 5. Compliance
Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

## 6. Review
This policy is reviewed annually or after significant changes.
